NIGO at the Design Museum

Yesterday, we went to see the NIGO exhibition at the Design Museum. I can’t say I’m super familiar with his work, but I’m obviously aware of his brand Bape.

I liked the exhibition, and there was lots to see. However, if I’m completely honest, I think what I enjoyed the most were the items from his personal collection that had inspired him, like Game & Watch, Star Wars action figures etc. Such nostalgia!
