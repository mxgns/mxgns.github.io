Back at the Proms

I love the BBC Proms and the special atmosphere in Royal Albert Hall. Some years, I’ve had a season tickets and some years we just buy tickets for a few specific proms. This year is a year we’re doing the latter, and when booking, I tend to pick some big, mainstream pieces.

Beethoven’s 9th, like yesterday, is always a good bet, and that proved true this year too.

Tonight, it’s time for our second (and last) prom this year: Also Sprach Zaratustra.
